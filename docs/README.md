# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This README provides a concise summary of OctoAcme's project management processes and direct links to the detailed process documents stored in the docs/ folder. It is intended as a landing page for all contributors, new team members, and stakeholders seeking to understand OctoAcme's lightweight, documented approach to project delivery.

## Brief summary

OctoAcme follows a lightweight, iterative project management approach that emphasizes customer value, clear ownership, and measurable outcomes.

### Project Initiation
Projects begin with a focused initiation (one‑pager or project charter) that documents objectives, stakeholders, success criteria, and key constraints. This ensures alignment before planning begins.

### Project Planning
Planning phase includes defining scope, breaking work into milestones, and creating a work breakdown structure (WBS). Capacity planning and risk identification occur at this stage to establish a realistic delivery timeline.

### Execution and Tracking
During execution, teams track progress using prioritized backlogs and regular cadenced communication: PM + PdM weekly syncs, delivery team standups, and monthly stakeholder updates. Backlog items include acceptance criteria, and status is tracked with clear completion metrics. Daily standups surface blockers and cross-team dependencies.

### Risks and Communication
Risk management and communication are explicit: risks are recorded in a Risk Register with owners and mitigations, cross‑team dependencies are surfaced on project boards, and escalation paths are defined. Communication cadence includes weekly syncs, monthly status reports, and ad-hoc escalations as needed.

### Release and Deployment
Releases follow a defined checklist covering code freeze, testing, security review, documentation, and go-live readiness. A clear release owner ensures accountability and smooth deployment to production.

### Retrospectives and Continuous Improvement
After each release or major milestone, the team conducts a retrospective to capture lessons learned, celebrate successes, and identify process improvements. Actions from retrospectives feed back into future iterations.

### Roles and Personas
OctoAcme defines clear roles (Product Manager, Project Manager, Delivery Lead, Contributors) with RACI matrices to establish decision-making authority and accountability across the organization.
